Four AI Agents Working For You

Powered by Claude AI — each agent handles a specific part of the workflow

Thread Extraction Agent

Reads forwarded emails, analyzes context, and identifies actionable work (tasks + requests). Outputs two lists: internal tasks with owners, and external requests with confidence scores, types, and due dates.

Triggered: When you forward an email

Autopilot Engine (Assist+)

Evaluates policy rules and decides: AUTO_SEND, DRAFT_ONLY, or BLOCKED. Checks confidence thresholds, trust seeding, business hours, daily caps, and cooldowns.

Triggered: When auto-extract is enabled

Reply Classification Agent

Interprets client replies and classifies them as APPROVED, REJECTED, or AMBIGUOUS. Updates request status automatically and flags unclear responses for human review.

Triggered: When client replies to a request

Nudge Generation Agent

Creates contextual follow-up emails based on request details, days waiting, and reminder count. Escalates tone appropriately while respecting a 4-hour anti-spam rule.

Triggered: Manual nudge or scheduled reminder

All AI decisions are logged in ai_decision_logs for full transparency and debugging

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What is TaskAt?

TaskAt is an email-first client request and approval system. You forward a client email → TaskAt turns it into a trackable request (Upload, Approve, Answer, Confirm, Schedule, Review, Checklist, or Rating) → your client receives it in their inbox, completes it, and you see the result in your dashboard. No portals. No passwords for clients.

How does the email threading work?

When you forward an email with a thread history, TaskAt reads and stores the full conversation. Clients see the original context in their request. When they reply, TaskAt routes responses to the correct request—even if the subject line changes or they reply from a different device.

What is AI Operator — Assist+ (Beta)?

Assist+ is our optional AI layer that auto-sends safe Upload + Answer requests when confident. Approval requests (decisions, sign-offs) always require your review. You can enable/disable per workspace, and all auto-sends are logged with reasoning.

What happens during the 14-day free pilot?

You get full access to all features with limits: 50 requests, 10 active clients, and 100 AI analyses. We help you set up, migrate existing threads if needed, and check in weekly. At the end, you choose to upgrade or stay in view-only mode (no sending, but you keep access to everything).

Is TaskAt secure?

SOC 2 Type II: Currently in audit preparation (Q2 2025 target). We've implemented Trust Services Criteria controls for security, availability, and confidentiality.

Encryption: TLS 1.3 in transit, AES-256 at rest. Files virus-scanned (ClamAV) before storage. Portal tokens bcrypt-hashed.

Data handling: Forwarded emails stored for extraction and thread linking. 90-day retention by default (configurable). Role-based access control. We never sell or share your data.

Infrastructure: Hosted on AWS (US regions). Rate limiting, DDoS protection, and 24/7 monitoring.

What is an active client?

A client becomes "active" when you send them at least one request. Clients with no open requests in the past 30 days don't count toward your limit.

What happens after my pilot ends?

After 14 days (or when limits are reached), sending pauses but you keep full access to view your dashboard, client history, and all uploaded files. You can stay in view-only mode or upgrade anytime to resume sending.
